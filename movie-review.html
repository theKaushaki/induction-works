About the Film

Dead Poets Society (1989) is more than a film—it’s a philosophy. The story of John Keating, an unorthodox English teacher, igniting a revolution among students of Welton Academy. Through the verses of Whitman, Frost, and Thoreau, they learn to challenge tradition, embrace passion, and live deliberately.
